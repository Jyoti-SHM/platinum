Near By Attraction

Hotel Platinum Inn places you in the heart of Mumbai with some of the city’s most iconic landmarks just a short distance away. Take a relaxing walk along the beautiful Marine Drive, or visit the sacred Babulnath Temple and the famous Mahalaxmi Temple. The stunning Haji Ali Dargah on the sea and the lively sands of Girgaon Chowpatty are also nearby. History lovers can explore the Mani Bhavan Gandhi Museum, while the unique sight of Dhobi Ghat offers a glimpse into Mumbai’s vibrant daily life. You can also plan a short drive to the majestic Gateway of India, or make a day trip to Juhu Beach and Siddhivinayak Temple, which are a bit further but worth the visit.
